AuthService Design

AuthService handles all user authentication for the platform.

Token lifecycle:
Tokens are valid for 24 hours from the time of issue.
Tokens can only be refreshed within the last 30 minutes before expiry.
Logout invalidates a token immediately by inserting it into the revoked_tokens table.
The revoked_tokens table is checked on every validation request.

Security policy:
After 5 consecutive failed login attempts, the account is locked.
Locked accounts remain locked for 15 minutes before automatically unlocking.
Passwords must be at least 8 characters.
