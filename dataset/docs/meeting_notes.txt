Engineering Decisions — Q1

Payment limits:
The team decided the minimum charge amount is $0.50 to avoid Stripe fees on micro-transactions.
The maximum single charge is $9,999.99 to limit fraud exposure.
Refunds are only accepted within 30 days of the original transaction.

Reliability:
PaymentService will retry failed Stripe calls up to 3 times with a 2-second delay between attempts.
If all 3 retries fail, the charge is marked as failed and the user is notified.

Rate limiting:
The API Gateway enforces a limit of 100 requests per minute per IP address.
Requests exceeding the limit receive a 429 response.

Data retention:
Transaction records are retained indefinitely for accounting purposes.
Revoked tokens are purged from the revoked_tokens table after 30 days.
